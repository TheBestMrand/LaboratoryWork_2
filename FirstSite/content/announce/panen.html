    Kharkiv international readings of Pandoniniv
    "Traveler through three worlds Hryhoriy Skovoroda: Skovoroda's
    work in the context of world philosophy for the 300th anniversary of his birth"


    On October 1, 2022, the Kharkiv international readings of the Pannadyniv will take place
    "Hryhoriy Skovoroda, a traveler through three worlds: Skovoroda's work in the context of world
    philosophy for the 300th anniversary of his birth."


    The following questions are planned to be considered during the readings:

    • Ukrainian Skovorodiana: a study of the work of H.S. Skovoroda in Ukraine 1991–2021;
    • ancient ideas and motives in the philosophy and poetry of H.S. Skovoroda. "Bible lover Hryhoriy Skovoroda";
    • Frying pan as a Christian thinker;
    • H.S. Skovoroda and his philosophy in the spiritual space of European rationalism..

    There will also be a panel discussion "Think continuously to know yourself" (H.S. Skovoroda). What will be Ukraine's future?


    Working languages ​​of the conference: Ukrainian, English.


    To participate in the readings, it is necessary to send an application by September 15, 2022 to the e-mail
    skovoroda.m@gmail.com i.v.karpenko@karazin.ua.
